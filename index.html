What are Youth Science Communication Workshops?

Youth Science Communication Workshops provides middle and high school students an ingress into environmental science and digital composition. Over the course of a single day, our participants investigate a topic, gather data, and use basic HTML coding to communicate and circulate their findings. As a result, our participants learn methods for communicating science while developing digital literacy. At the end of the day, our participants return home with a project link they can share with family and friends. In our session, you'll:

  • explore nature or investigate a topic
  • gather image and sound data
  • develop a webtext using HTML
  • inform environmental policy

    • And best of all...It's Free!
