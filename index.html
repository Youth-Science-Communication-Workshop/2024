What is my Nature Outing?

My Nature Outing provides middle and high school students an ingress into environmental education and computer science. Over the course of a single day, our students investigate nature, gather data, and use basic HTML and CSS coding to communicate and circulate their findings. As a result, students learn methods for communicating science while developing basic coding literacy. Additionally, students return home with a URL link to their science communication document that can easily be circulated among family and friends. In our session, you'll:

  • take a walk in nature
  • gather image and sound data
  • develop a webtext using HTML
  • inform environmental policy

    • And best of all...It's Free!
